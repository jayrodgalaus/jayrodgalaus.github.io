DRRMIS CLEARS

CLEARS is a module of DRRMIS under DOST CAR, designed to indicate the risks and likelihood of a mountainside or slope collapsing based on factors like angle, material, and weather conditions. It includes an API that integrates with a mobile application used by LGUs and experts to assess slopes and determine their suitability for construction.
